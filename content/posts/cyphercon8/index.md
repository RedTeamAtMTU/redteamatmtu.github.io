The RedTeam from [Michigan Tech](https://www.mtu.edu) just finished wrapping up at [CypherCon 8](https://cyphercon.com/)!

Thanks to everyone who helped out with running the event, all the other wards for attending, and [Michael Goetzman](https://goetzman.com/) for putting it all together.

We all had a great time hosting our [Access Control Village](https://accessvillage.net) at CypherCon again this year, we learned even more this second time around!

This year we had more space, enabling even more attendees to participate in our labs geared towards teaching often-overlooked vulnerabilities in [Physical Access Control Systems](https://www.avigilon.com/blog/physical-access-control) and locking hardware.

Some convention attendees even brought their own tools to find new [0-day vulnerabilities](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zero-day_vulnerability) in our hotel lab hardware.

We're extremely grateful to Michael and our University for making this trip possible, and are excited about coming back next year!
